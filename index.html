How to Play

Your goal is to guess the correct logical statement in 6 tries.

Enter statements using the keyboard or on-screen buttons.

Special characters: ∀(a), ∃(e), ∈(b), ℝ(r), ℕ(n), ≤(l), ≥(g), ≠(!)

Click Enter to submit a row.

Green tiles are correct; gray tiles are incorrect.

Easy mode: one variable per expression
